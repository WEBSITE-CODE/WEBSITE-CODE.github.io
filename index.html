How to play "balls and hoops".

To play this victorian game, you will need:

  • A few very small hoops
  • A few small sticks or poles to ring the hoop around
  • A ball

    • Instructions:

    1. Poke the poles randomly into the ground.

    2. Hold a few hoops in one hand, and a ball in the other.

    3. Throw the ball as high up as you can, and try to hoop as many rings around the poles as you can. Catch the ball on its way down.

    4. Note down your score and improve on it.

    Top tip: Throw up the ball as high as you can, but straight up so you don't have a problem when trying to catch it.
